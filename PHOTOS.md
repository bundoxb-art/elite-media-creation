─────────────────────────────────────────────────
 TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Use JPG format for photos (smaller file size)
• Recommended size: 1280px wide minimum
• Keep each photo under 2MB for fast loading
• The site auto-hides the grey placeholder boxes
  once a real photo is placed in the right location

─────────────────────────────────────────────────
 DEPLOY TO VERCEL AFTER ADDING PHOTOS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Go to vercel.com
2. Drag the entire "elite-media-creation" folder
3. Click Deploy
4. Done! ✅
